About


My name is Nicola Stettler, vonwildsau is my nom du pilote. The name originated in 2011 when I started racing Cyclekarts; which are handmade single-seater cars built to resemble race cars from the 1920s. I built the first electric cyclekart in 2014 but have since left the engines behind and enjoy building bicycles, Hammocks and other goods.

With a brief 3 year stint as a stage manager he now builds temporary structures for several art organizations and by pure chance seems to be bartending for swiss circuses every summer.

Nico dropped out of college; focusing on GIS analysis and Web Based Mapping. He is available for hire in the Non-Profit and Environmental Sector.
